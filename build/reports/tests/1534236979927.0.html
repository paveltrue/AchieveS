Sentence Frames
  • Agreement
    • The reason I believe _____ is because _____.
    • I agree with _____ because of _____.
    • I agree that _____ is correct because my experience with _____ confirms it.
    • _____ is right about _____ because recent studies have shown that _____.
    • I agree because _____ seems logical in this situation.
    • I agree with the statement that _____ because I can't think of a time when this would not be true.
  • Disagreement
    • I don't agree with _____ because _____.
    • I disagree with the statement that _____ because _____.
    • I think _____ is mistaken because of _____.
    • I see it another way because of _____.
    • I can see that; however, I disagree because of _____.
    • I disagree with the view that _____ because recent research has shown _____.
  • Mixed Feelings
    • I agree with _____, but on the other hand _____.
    • Though I understand that _____, I still believe that _____.
    • I have mixed feelings about the claim that _____. On one hand, I agree that _____. On the other hand, I'm not sure if _____.
    • My feelings on the issue are mixed. I support the position that _____, but I find the argument about _____ and the research on _____ to be equally convincing.
    • Although I agree with the idea that _____ up to a point, I cannot accept the overall conclusion that _____.
